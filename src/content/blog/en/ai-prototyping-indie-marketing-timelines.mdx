Marketing timelines assume **assets exist**. When prototypes arrive **48 hours sooner**, your content calendar shifts left—but **player expectations** shift too: more WIP footage, more candid tool talk, less “big reveal only” mystique.

## What compresses

- **Vertical slice** availability for streamers.
- **Screenshot iteration** (lighting passes, HUD tweaks).
- **Pitch deck** diagrams if you fundraise.

## What does not compress

- **Trust building** with communities.
- **Platform policy** reviews for commercial releases.
- **Localization** and **accessibility** hardening.

## Practical GTM stack for web-first indies

1. **Stable play URL** + changelog.
2. **Short-loop social** (GIF > essay).
3. **Hub presence** on [vibe-coded games](/vibe-coded-games) with tool tags.
4. **Developer story** on [developers](/developers).

## FAQ

**Wishlist before polish?** Only if your page sets honest expectations.

**Influencers?** Lead with **30s hook**, not roadmap fantasies.
