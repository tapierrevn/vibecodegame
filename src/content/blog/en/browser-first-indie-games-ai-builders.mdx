**Browser-first** does not mean “lesser”—it means **friction budget** spent on gameplay, not installers. AI tooling lowers the cost of **UI + glue**, so more teams ship **playable URLs** before greenlight politics begin.

## Why players benefit

- **Instant try** on lunch break.
- **Cross-platform** without store gates for experiments.
- **Iteration** visible refresh-by-refresh during early access.

## Why builders benefit

- **Analytics** simpler at small scale.
- **Embedding** in jams, hubs, and portfolios with one iframe.

## Constraints to respect

- **Storage limits** and **audio autoplay** policies.
- **Safari** testing non-optional for global reach.
