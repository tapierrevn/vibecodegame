Discovery is **balkanized**: TikTok spikes, Itch buries, Steam ignores your `.html` unless wrapped. **Curated hubs** and **maker transparency** are rising as antidotes—players want “**play now**” plus “**built how?**”

## Channels that still convert

- **Short video** with three seconds of uninterrupted gameplay before talking.
- **Curated hubs** with honest tool tags—see [vibe-coded games](/vibe-coded-games).
- **Newsletters** from communities that respect your inbox.

## Signals we watch

- **Tool-literate players** asking smarter questions in comments.
- **Cross-posting** between jams, hubs, and personal portfolios shrinking.
- **Credit screens** listing assistants and human roles—becoming standard.

## Advice for listers

- One **primary URL** everywhere.
- **GIF + controls** in first screen of any social post.
- Link your **stack writeup** on [AI tools](/ai-tools) pages you actually used.

## FAQ

**Is SEO dead?** No—**long-tail** searches (“browser roguelike cursor”) still work with good copy.

**Do I need Discord?** Only if you will **staff** it; otherwise use issue trackers or email.
