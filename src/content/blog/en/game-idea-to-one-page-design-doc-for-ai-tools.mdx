Great prompts start as **boring documents**. One page forces **clarity**; clarity reduces hallucinated features.

## Sections (in order)

1. **Elevator** — 2 sentences.
2. **Core loop** — bullet timeline of one round.
3. **Inputs & platforms** — keyboard, touch, gamepad?
4. **Entities** — player, enemies, pickups; max counts.
5. **Progression** — what changes between rounds?
6. **Non-goals** — explicit cuts.
7. **Acceptance tests** — verifiable sentences.

## Example acceptance test

> Given two players join room `ABCD`, when player one presses **Space**, round starts within **200ms** and timer counts down from **60**.

## Why non-goals matter

AI fills silence. Tell it **what not to build** (no accounts, no shop, no story mode).

## FAQ

**Length?** One page, 12pt, no appendix. Link art references instead of embedding essays.

**Versioning?** `design-v3.md` in repo; mention version in prompts.
