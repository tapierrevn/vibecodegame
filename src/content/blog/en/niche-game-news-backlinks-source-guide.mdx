**Backlinks** follow **credibility**. Niche outlets want **primary sources**: builds that run, founders who reply, and facts that check out.

## Make a /press page

- **Factsheet** (bullet studio, genre, platforms, price).
- **Logo & screenshots** ZIP.
- **Contact** with SLA you can keep (e.g. 48h).

## Be quotable

Share **measurable** claims: load time, player count order-of-magnitude, jam placement—avoid unverifiable superlatives.

## Embeds and exclusives

Offer **GIF-first** assets; journalists live on deadlines.

## Ethical outreach

- Personalize **one** sentence proving you read their column.
- No mass mail merge guilt trips.

## Tie-ins

List where you **ship** on [vibe-coded games](/vibe-coded-games); point to [developers](/developers) for bio depth.

## FAQ

**Follow up?** Once, politely; then move on.

**Harassment?** Never—reputation is a long game.
