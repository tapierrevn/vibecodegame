**YGG Play** backs a simple idea: **players and builders both win** when games are easy to try and **honest about how they were made**. Vibe-coding is a **method**, not a genre—arcade, puzzle, and narrative games all belong.

## What we optimize for

- **Fast play** paths and clear controls.
- **Builder identity** through [developer profiles](/developers).
- **Tool literacy** via [AI tools](/ai-tools) pages you can cite.
- **Deadlines** that ship art via [game jams](/game-jams).

## For listers

Bring a **stable URL**, **media**, and **truthful tags**. We surface games to players who want **new mechanics** and **new workflows**, not only AAA polish.

## For players

Expect **browser-first** experiments alongside downloadable titles. Use filters and follow creators whose taste you trust.

## FAQ

**Is everything AI-generated?** No—**AI-assisted** spans a spectrum; read credits and tool tags.

**How to contribute?** Ship something listable on [vibe-coded games](/vibe-coded-games) and tell your story.
