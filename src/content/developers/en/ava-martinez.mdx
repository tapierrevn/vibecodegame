### Simulated Interview with Ava Martinez

**Q1: What kind of games are you most excited to build right now?**  
I am building high-energy games that combine **arcade** immediacy with **endless-runner** momentum, then layer in **fighting** depth and **multiplayer** chaos. I want sessions to be instantly fun in 60 seconds but still reward mastery over weeks.

**Q2: How does your role as Creative Director shape your approach?**  
As Creative Director, I spend a lot of time protecting the core feeling of "speed plus style." I align art, controls, audio, and progression around that promise so every decision supports fast readability, expressive inputs, and replay value for solo and social players.

**Q3: What does your AI-assisted workflow look like each week?**  
I use **ChatGPT** for rapid ideation on event modifiers, combo challenge prompts, and onboarding copy. I use **Cursor** to accelerate prototype iteration with designers and engineers, and **GitHub Copilot** to speed up repetitive gameplay scaffolding while we focus on polish and balancing.

**Q4: How do your preferred genres influence the final design?**  
The **arcade** and **endless-runner** tags keep us strict about low-friction controls and instant restart loops. The **fighting** tag pushes us toward readable combat interactions and spacing decisions, while **multiplayer** requires robust comeback systems and fairness under latency.

**Q5: What is one challenge you recently solved?**  
We had a version where advanced players dominated online rounds too quickly, which hurt retention for newer players. We added dynamic matchmaking rules, clearer telegraphs, and adaptive scoring multipliers, then used AI-supported test scenario generation to validate edge cases before launch.

**Q6: What are you aiming to build next?**  
I want to ship a seasonal multiplayer mode that blends endless-runner routing decisions with short tactical duels. The long-term plan is a living arcade ecosystem where community-created challenges coexist with curated events and still preserve tight, skill-based game feel.
