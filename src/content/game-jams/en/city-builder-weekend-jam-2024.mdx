## Theme

**Grow Under Pressure**. Build a city-building experience where every expansion introduces a meaningful trade-off.

## Event Parameters

- Duration: 48 hours
- Team size: 1-3 people
- Platforms: WebGL preferred, desktop accepted
- Mandatory feature: one district-level decision with visible long-term impact

## Prize Breakdown

Total support package value: **EUR 8,000**

- Best Strategic Depth - EUR 3,000
- Best UI Readability - EUR 2,000
- Best Audio Atmosphere - EUR 1,500
- Community Choice - EUR 1,500

## Submission Workflow

1. Upload playable build and source summary.
2. Provide one map seed used for evaluation.
3. Include a balancing note describing your economy loop.
4. Submit by 20:00 CEST on June 16.

## Judging Dimensions

- Strategy clarity
- Information hierarchy and UX
- Replayability and balancing
- Production completeness

## Submission Highlights

- **Block by Block** - a district zoning sim where transit debt impacts citizen happiness.
- **Harborline** - a coastal logistics builder with weather-driven resource volatility.
- **Night Shift Mayor** - a pressure-management city sim with strict 5-minute rounds.

## Final Results

- **Winner:** Harborline (Team Delta Harbor)
- **2nd Place:** Block by Block (Team Gridform)
- **Community Choice:** Night Shift Mayor (Team Owl Clock)

The jury emphasized Harborline's clean UI and excellent risk-reward balancing across short sessions.
