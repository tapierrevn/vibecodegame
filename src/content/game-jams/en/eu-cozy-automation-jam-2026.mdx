## Theme

**Small Worlds, Smart Systems**. Participants create short-form games where automation helps the player nurture, craft, or restore a space.

## Format

This jam runs as a hybrid event:

- In-person hub at MotionLab Berlin
- Remote participation via Discord and daily office hours

## Rules

- Team size: 1-4 people
- Engine/tooling: open choice
- Accessibility requirement: include at least one accessibility option (text size, key rebinding, color mode, etc.)
- Content requirement: no explicit violence or hateful content

## Timeline

- **Theme reveal:** Sep 18, 17:00 CEST
- **Mentor clinic:** Sep 21 and Sep 24
- **Final upload cutoff:** Sep 27, 21:00 CEST
- **Demo night in Berlin:** Oct 2

## Prize Categories

- Best Cozy Loop - EUR 2,500
- Best Automation Design - EUR 2,000
- Best Accessibility - EUR 1,500
- Community Favorite - EUR 1,000

Additional sponsor rewards include playtest sessions with partner studios and Steam page review feedback.

## Submission Checklist

1. Upload build and screenshots to your Itch.io page.
2. Add design notes documenting your automation system.
3. Include a one-page controls reference.
4. Mark the entry as solo or team, and list contributors.

## Judging Rubric

- Clarity of systems
- Emotional tone consistency
- Accessibility and UX
- Technical execution
