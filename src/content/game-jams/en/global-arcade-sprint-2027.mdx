## Theme

**One More Run**. Build an arcade experience where the core loop is clear in under 20 seconds and players immediately want to retry.

## Eligibility and Team Rules

- Solo developers and teams up to 5 members are welcome.
- Entries must be built during the jam window.
- Reused assets are allowed if disclosed on your submission page.
- AI-assisted workflows are allowed, but the game design and final integration decisions must be your own.

## Schedule

- **Kickoff stream:** Feb 5, 18:00 UTC
- **Mid-jam playtest checkpoint:** Feb 10, 19:00 UTC
- **Submission deadline:** Feb 14, 23:59 UTC
- **Community voting:** Feb 15-20
- **Jury finals showcase:** Feb 23

## Submission Process

1. Publish your game on Itch.io with downloadable build and web build (if available).
2. Include a 60-90 second gameplay video.
3. Add a short postmortem covering core mechanic, tools used, and technical risks solved.
4. Tag your page with `global-arcade-sprint-2027`.

## Judging Criteria

- **Gameplay Loop (40%)**: Is the loop immediately understandable and replayable?
- **Game Feel (25%)**: Input responsiveness, juice, and audiovisual feedback.
- **Innovation (20%)**: Fresh mechanic or twist within arcade constraints.
- **Polish (15%)**: Stability, clarity, and onboarding quality.
