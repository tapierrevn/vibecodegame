## Theme

**Broken Rules, Better Games**. Teams were encouraged to intentionally violate one common design convention and build around the consequences.

## Participation Snapshot

- 428 registered participants
- 136 submitted prototypes
- 22 countries represented

## Prize Pool

Total: **CAD 15,000**

- Grand Jury Winner: CAD 7,000
- Runner-up: CAD 4,000
- Experimental Design Award: CAD 2,000
- Best Student Team: CAD 2,000

## Submission Process Used

Participants submitted:

1. playable build (web or desktop),
2. a 2-minute narrated walkthrough,
3. a one-page design intent note,
4. a known-issues list for judges.

## Judging Criteria

- Design risk and originality (35%)
- Playability and onboarding (30%)
- Technical execution (20%)
- Presentation quality (15%)

## Submission Highlights

- **Signal//Lost** - asynchronous co-op puzzle where each player sees different rule sets.
- **Parallax Drift** - racing prototype with delayed input prediction as core mechanic.
- **Borrowed HP** - action deckbuilder where health can be traded for rule changes.

## Final Results

- **Winner:** Signal//Lost (Team Frostbyte)
- **Runner-up:** Borrowed HP (Team Mantle)
- **Experimental Design Award:** Parallax Drift (Team Slipstream)

Judges praised Signal//Lost for turning communication friction into compelling cooperative play.
