## Studio Overview

8bit Collective builds replayable games where mechanical precision meets build experimentation. Their projects emphasize quick onboarding with long-term mastery through layered combat and progression systems.

## AI Workflow

The studio combines complementary AI tools:

- **ChatGPT** for ideation of encounter systems and progression economy alternatives.
- **Claude** for design reasoning, balancing scenarios, and implementation planning.

## Production Philosophy

8bit Collective runs a test-heavy pipeline where each gameplay system is validated against readability, fairness, and run-to-run variety. Their goal is to make each play session feel both familiar and surprising.
