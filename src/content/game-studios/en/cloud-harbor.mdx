## Studio Overview

Cloud Harbor creates management and city-building games with a strong focus on economy feedback loops, optimization depth, and long-horizon progression.

## AI Workflow

- **Claude** helps evaluate complex simulation and balancing decisions.
- **Cursor** accelerates implementation and optimization tasks.
- **ChatGPT** is used for scenario ideation and content support.

## Production Philosophy

Cloud Harbor emphasizes systems that remain understandable even as they scale. Releases are staged to preserve performance, clarity, and player agency.
