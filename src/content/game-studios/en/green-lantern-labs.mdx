## Studio Overview

Green Lantern Labs develops accessible strategy-forward experiences with clear objectives, peaceful pacing, and long-tail progression. Their work blends simulation logic with player-friendly interfaces.

## AI Workflow

- **ChatGPT** is used for content scaffolding and event variant generation.
- **Claude** is used to stress-test economy and simulation systems before release.

## Production Philosophy

The studio focuses on maintainable systems and iterative player feedback. Each milestone improves onboarding, pacing, and scenario diversity without increasing interface complexity.
