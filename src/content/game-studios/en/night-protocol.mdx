## Studio Overview

Night Protocol designs mission-driven games where player choices and tactical planning shape the run. Their titles mix cinematic direction with systems-heavy encounter design.

## AI Workflow

- **Claude** helps evaluate mission logic and narrative consistency across branches.
- **ChatGPT** supports ideation for mission variants and diegetic writing.
- **Cursor** streamlines implementation and rapid iteration cycles.

## Production Philosophy

The studio values structure and replayability equally. Features are shipped in modular slices so narrative and gameplay systems can evolve without destabilizing core loops.
