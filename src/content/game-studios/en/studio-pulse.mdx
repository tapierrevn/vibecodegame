## Studio Overview

Studio Pulse builds games around short, high-energy sessions where clarity and responsiveness come first. The team is known for structuring production around weekly playtest loops and instrumented balancing passes.

## AI Workflow

The studio uses AI tooling throughout concept, prototyping, and tuning:

- **ChatGPT** for content ideation, challenge variants, and progression copy.
- **Cursor** for rapid gameplay iteration and refactoring support during feature spikes.
- **GitHub Copilot** for repetitive gameplay scripting and utility layer implementation.

## Production Philosophy

Studio Pulse ships in compact milestones, validates with community telemetry, then expands through event-driven updates. Their core principle is to keep game feel tight while continuously layering depth for returning players.
