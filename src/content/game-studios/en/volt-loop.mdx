## Studio Overview

Volt Loop specializes in velocity-based gameplay where readability and reactive controls are central to player retention. The team builds around replay loops, leaderboard systems, and challenge-based progression.

## AI Workflow

- **Claude** supports design reviews and edge-case reasoning for mechanics.
- **Cursor** accelerates gameplay iteration and technical cleanup during production sprints.

## Production Philosophy

The studio uses a metric-first cadence: instrument, test, refine. Every major release cycle includes difficulty tuning, UX polish, and latency-sensitive control improvements.
