Byte Brawlers is a competitive 2D team fighter focused on coordinated pushes, gadget timing, and objective control.

## Gameplay

Matches are played in fast rounds where two squads contest map objectives while managing cooldowns and ult economy. Team comp decisions matter: bruisers open space, supports sustain pressure, and flankers punish bad positioning.

## Key Features

- 3v3 objective modes with quick round turnover
- Distinct brawler classes with unique ultimates
- Gadget system for zoning, displacement, and setup plays
- Ranked and custom lobbies for competitive or casual sessions

## Why It Stands Out

Unlike pure duel fighters, Byte Brawlers emphasizes communication and tempo swings. Well-timed ult chains can flip a match instantly, making teamwork and adaptation the core skill.
