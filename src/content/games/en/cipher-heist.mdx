Cipher Heist is a stealth-puzzle experience where route planning, timing windows, and system manipulation are the heart of every mission.

## Gameplay

You infiltrate high-security facilities, map patrol loops, and chain silent interactions to reach extraction undetected. Levels are designed as logic spaces, so success comes from observation and clean execution rather than brute force.

## Key Features

- Multi-path stealth sandboxes with layered security systems
- Environmental puzzles tied to cameras, locks, and power grids
- Alert-state escalation that changes enemy routes dynamically
- Mission scoring based on noise, visibility, and completion speed

## Why It Stands Out

Cipher Heist rewards patience and planning. Every successful run feels authored by the player because solutions emerge from understanding systems, not following a single scripted path.

## AI-Assisted Development

`Cursor` helped structure mission scripting and trigger logic, while `Claude` supported puzzle flow reviews to keep challenge curves fair and readable.
