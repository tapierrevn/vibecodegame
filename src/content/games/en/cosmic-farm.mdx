Cosmic Farm is a cozy farming simulation set across small alien worlds, built for relaxed progression and expressive base customization.

## Gameplay

You cultivate biome-specific crops, befriend local creatures, and automate simple production chains to expand your interplanetary homestead. Sessions are intentionally low-pressure, with optional goals that let you play at your own pace.

## Key Features

- Multiple planets with unique weather and soil mechanics
- Crop, crafting, and habitat systems that interconnect naturally
- Decorative building tools for personalized farm layouts
- Bite-sized quests and seasonal events for steady progression

## Why It Stands Out

Cosmic Farm keeps the loop calm without becoming shallow. It mixes light optimization with cozy worldbuilding so players can choose between efficiency and creativity each session.

## AI-Assisted Development

`Claude` was used to iterate quest text and progression beats, while `Copilot` accelerated implementation of inventory helpers and farming simulation utilities.
