Dungeon Dash is a run-based roguelite RPG that combines pattern-driven combat with high-variance loot decisions.

## Gameplay

Each descent is procedurally assembled from combat rooms, trap corridors, and elite encounters. You build around found gear and relic synergies, deciding whether to play safely for consistency or push deeper for rare rewards.

## Key Features

- Procedural dungeon layouts with branching risk/reward paths
- Class-like build identities created through relic combinations
- Boss fights with learnable telegraphs and escalating phases
- Meta progression that unlocks new run modifiers and options

## Why It Stands Out

Dungeon Dash balances accessibility and depth: controls are immediate, but long-term mastery comes from build adaptation and encounter knowledge.

## AI-Assisted Development

`ChatGPT` supported early item and relic ideation, while `Claude` helped refine enemy behavior specs and run-economy balancing notes.
