Forest Keepers is a co-op strategy defense game where squads protect living biomes from corruption waves.

## Gameplay

Teams coordinate resource routes, place defenses, and rotate between lanes as threats evolve in real time. Match success depends on communication, role coverage, and timing defensive cooldowns across multiple fronts.

## Key Features

- Cooperative lane-defense gameplay for up to four players
- Biome-specific objectives that change tactical priorities
- Upgrade trees for structures, utility tools, and support abilities
- Dynamic enemy mutations that force mid-match adaptation

## Why It Stands Out

Forest Keepers blends tactical planning with multiplayer urgency. It rewards teams that share information and adjust quickly instead of relying on one fixed build order.

## AI-Assisted Development

`Copilot` accelerated systems code for wave scheduling and upgrades, while `Cursor` improved iteration speed on co-op state sync and balance passes.
