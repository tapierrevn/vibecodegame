Mecha Rally is an action-racing hybrid where customizable mechs battle for position across physics-heavy arena tracks.

## Gameplay

Races combine boost management, weapon timing, and terrain interaction. You tune chassis stats between events, then adapt your driving line and combat choices based on track hazards and rival loadouts.

## Key Features

- Arena circuits with shortcuts, hazards, and verticality
- Modular mech customization for speed, armor, and handling
- Mid-race combat tools that create high-risk overtakes
- Event ladders with escalating AI and mixed rule sets

## Why It Stands Out

Mecha Rally treats racing and combat as one system: positioning creates attack opportunities, and offense reshapes race strategy.

## AI-Assisted Development

`ChatGPT` supported early tuning frameworks and event rule concepts, while `Gemini` assisted with telemetry review summaries and physics parameter experimentation.
