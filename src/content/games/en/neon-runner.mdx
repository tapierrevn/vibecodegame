Neon Runner is all about **flow**: one-thumb controls, instant restarts, and a synthwave presentation tuned for high-speed clarity.

## Gameplay

You sprint through procedural lanes, chaining near-misses and pulse pickups to push your multiplier while avoiding late-run pattern overload. Runs are short, but scoring depth comes from route commitment and split-second correction.

## Key Features

- Endless-runner loop designed for rapid retries
- Score multiplier system that rewards confident movement
- Procedural obstacle grammar for high replay variety
- Daily challenge seeds for competitive high-score sessions

## Why It Stands Out

Neon Runner keeps friction low and mastery high. New players can jump in instantly, while advanced players optimize lines and tempo for leaderboard gains.
