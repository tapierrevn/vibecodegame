Neon Runner is all about **flow**: one-thumb controls, instant restarts, and a soundtrack that matches your speed. The world procedurally stitches neon strips and hazards so no two runs feel identical.

We leaned on AI assistants for level grammar ideas and balance spreadsheets, then hand-tuned every animation curve so the game still feels human-made.
