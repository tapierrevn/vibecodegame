Pixel Quest is a cozy pixel RPG with tight room-scale combat, rewarding exploration, and short-session dungeon progression.

## Gameplay

You clear handcrafted dungeon floors, decode enemy tells, and choose when to spend resources or push one room deeper. Hidden paths and optional objectives reward players who explore carefully instead of rushing the critical route.

## Key Features

- Readable pixel combat with pattern-based boss encounters
- Secret rooms, breakables, and optional mini-objectives
- Gear and charm system that supports different playstyles
- Compact runs ideal for quick sessions and repeat attempts

## Why It Stands Out

Pixel Quest mixes comfort and challenge well: visuals are nostalgic and warm, but encounter design still asks for deliberate timing and smart positioning.

## AI-Assisted Development

`ChatGPT` supported item and encounter ideation, while `Claude` helped tune tutorial pacing and readability for new players.
