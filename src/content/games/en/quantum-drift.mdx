Quantum Drift is a high-speed arcade racer built around time-shift mechanics, precision cornering, and short competitive runs.

## Gameplay

Each track challenges you to alternate between normal pace and controlled time dilation to optimize lines and recover from mistakes. Mastery comes from learning when to spend temporal charge for corner exits versus defensive correction.

## Key Features

- Time-bending driving mechanic with strategic resource use
- Compact tracks designed for leaderboard replayability
- Ghost racing and split tracking for lap optimization
- Vehicle handling profiles for different racing styles

## Why It Stands Out

Quantum Drift feels fresh because it turns time control into a skill expression tool, not a simple boost button. Fast races still leave room for deep optimization.
