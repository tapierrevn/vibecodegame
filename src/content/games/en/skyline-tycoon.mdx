Skyline Tycoon is a strategy-simulation game where you design and optimize a floating city economy under shifting demand and resource pressure.

## Gameplay

You zone districts, balance production chains, and manage transit throughput while responding to events that disrupt supply or labor. Sessions are structured for meaningful progress in short bursts, with long-term goals tied to city specialization.

## Key Features

- Layered city-economy simulation with clear feedback loops
- Modular district planning and infrastructure management
- Scenario events that force strategic trade-offs
- Sandbox and goal-driven modes for different player styles

## Why It Stands Out

Skyline Tycoon offers satisfying optimization depth without overwhelming onboarding. It gives players enough data to plan intelligently while keeping the interface readable.

## AI-Assisted Development

`Gemini` helped explore economic event scenarios and balancing narratives, while `Cursor` accelerated simulation-system refactors and UI iteration.
